Caveats and honest reporting
----------------------------
* Mutation-only HRD is a crude proxy.  Published genomic-scar
  based HRD scores (HRD-LOH, LST, TAI, Myriad score) perform
  noticeably better than binary mutation counts.
* The v17 genomic_features matrix only retains frequently
  mutated cancer genes, so 15 of the 19 intended HR-panel
  genes (PALB2, RAD51, RAD51B/C/D, BARD1, BRIP1, NBN,
  MRE11A, RAD50, FANCA/C/D2/E/F) are not represented.
  This truncation is expected to attenuate HRD-sensitivity
  correlations compared with the published literature.
* Published Spearman rho for Olaparib vs mutation-only HRD
  in cell line panels is typically -0.15 to -0.3; our
  estimates should be interpreted against that expectation.
* VE-821 and AZD6738 are in GDSC release 8.x but not in v17,
  so ATRi sensitivity is not assessed directly; KU-55933
  (ATMi) and AZD7762 (CHKi) are DDR-pathway stand-ins only.
